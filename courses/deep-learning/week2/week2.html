What happens in Week 2

In Week 2, the student begins to understand how the model actually learns. We move from just knowing the layers to understanding error, correction, optimization, and why training takes many steps before a network becomes useful.

Recommended approach

  • Start with Backpropagation first
  • Then move to Gradient Descent
  • Then study Loss Functions carefully
  • Review all 3 lectures together at the end
