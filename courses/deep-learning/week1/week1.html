What happens in Week 1

This week is designed to establish the conceptual foundations of deep learning before moving into more advanced topics. Students begin by understanding neural networks, layers, parameters, training, and why deep learning became so powerful.

Recommended approach

  • Start with Lecture 1 in order
  • Read the Arabic summary after each lecture
  • Review key concepts before moving on
  • Use glossary and quiz for active recall
